Šutka swimming pool attendence prediction

Welcome to Šutka swimming pool attendence predictor. This page predicts how many people will visit Šutka swimming pool each day. It uses machine learning algorithms that learns from historical data of pool attendance, reserved lines, weather and national holidays and predicts future attendence.

You can choose date in the calendar on the left and see the prediction in the chart below. Predictions are generated up to a week in to the future. For the dates in past you can compare real attendence with our predictions.

So far there are two different algorithms predicting attendence - each with the different color in chart below. You can toggle on/off the prediction by clicking on the name of algorithm on top of the chart.
